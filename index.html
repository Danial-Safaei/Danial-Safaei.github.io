About Me

I'm a PhD researcher at WMG, University of Warwick, working on Safe AI and Autonomous Driving Systems in collaboration with Siemens.

My research focuses on ensuring the safe deployment of AI in safety-critical autonomous systems through synthetic data fidelity assessment, scenario-based testing, and verification & validation methodologies. I'm contributing to the development of the world's largest autonomous vehicle scenario database (Safety Pool‚Ñ¢ with 270,000+ scenarios).

With a background in Computer Science and a Master's in Artificial Intelligence specializing in Reinforcement Learning, I bring 9 years of programming experience to cutting-edge research in computer vision, trajectory prediction, and machine learning for smart systems.
