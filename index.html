About Me

I'm a PhD student at the University of Warwick focused on cutting-edge research in generative AI and safety-critical scenario generation for autonomous vehicles.

My research combines the power of artificial intelligence with real-world safety validation, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in autonomous vehicle testing. I'm passionate about creating AI systems that not only work well but are also safe, reliable, and trustworthy.

With expertise spanning machine learning, probabilistic modeling, and computer vision, I'm committed to advancing the field of AI safety and making autonomous systems more robust and dependable.
