About Me

👋 Hello! I'm Danial Safaei, a passionate PhD student at the University of Warwick dedicated to advancing the field of artificial intelligence and autonomous systems.

🎯 My research focuses on generative AI and safety-critical scenario generation for autonomous vehicles. I'm particularly interested in how we can leverage cutting-edge AI techniques to create realistic and challenging test scenarios that ensure the safety and reliability of self-driving systems.

💡 I believe in the transformative power of AI and its potential to revolutionize transportation, making our roads safer and more efficient. My work combines theoretical research with practical applications, bridging the gap between academic innovation and real-world implementation.
